Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets through dream-sharing technology. Cobb is offered a chance to erase his criminal past if he can achieve the impossible task of planting an idea into someone's mind.

Actors

Main actors include Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.
